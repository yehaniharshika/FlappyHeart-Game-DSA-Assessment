  1. Press the Spacebar or the Up Arrow key to make the heart fly upward.
  2. Navigate the heart through the gaps in the pipes to collect points.
  3. You will earn 25 points for every pipe you successfully pass.
  4. If you hit a pipe or the ground, a Heart API Puzzle will appear, you must answer correctly to continue.
  5. You have only 10 seconds to solve the puzzle, A wrong answer or a timeout results in a Game Over.
  6. The game lasts for 60 seconds, making it a fast-paced competition to reach the highest score.
